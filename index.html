Obtaining Medical Cannabis in Colombia

Accessing medical cannabis in Colombia involves consulting a licensed doctor, obtaining a prescription, and registering it with approved providers. Patients can then buy regulated products through licensed pharmacies or clinics, ensuring compliance with medical and legal standards.
